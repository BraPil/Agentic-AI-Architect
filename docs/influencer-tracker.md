# Influencer and Influential Posts Tracker

## Purpose

This document tracks practitioners, creators, operators, and educators whose work is relevant to the
AI Architect knowledge surface.

It is a durable source registry for:

- people to follow
- high-signal posts to review
- recurring themes worth folding into Phase 1 education and later trend tracking

## Current Evidence Standard

This tracker now has three levels of maturity:

- reviewed public sources with summaries and tags
- tiered influencer prioritization for ongoing monitoring
- a structured companion registry for future machine ingestion

Many of the saved URLs are LinkedIn pages or LinkedIn posts.

Because those often require authenticated access, reviewed summaries should only be added when the
content is directly accessible or provided by the user. Until then, topic labels stay conservative and
are derived only from visible URL slugs, public metadata, or clearly accessible page titles.

Current operating rule for this phase:

- focus on ingestion and training readiness
- do not prune sources during the current ingestion pass
- revisit dormancy-based pruning later only if a source has been inactive for an extended period

## Status Key

- `captured` — URL saved in the tracker
- `pending-review` — saved but content not yet reviewed in detail
- `reviewed` — content inspected and summarized
- `monitor` — source should be revisited periodically
- `auth-blocked` — content likely requires authenticated access to inspect directly
