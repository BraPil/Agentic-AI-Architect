Our new course, "Agentic AI Engineering," contains 17 notebooks and a complete multi-agent project (because you can't learn to build production agents through notebooks only).

Notebooks are really powerful for teaching the core concepts quickly and easily to anyone. Which we can then use in the final project. You don't have to set up your environment (using Colab), or you can do so very easily with specific requirement packages.

Still, we also believe it's important to "sweat" in order to learn, and notebooks are thus not the entirety of the course. The main goal of the course is to build your own production-grade agent systems (deployed to the cloud with Docker and Cloud Run, managed with CI/CD) by leveraging the techniques we teach through notebooks.

We teach every core technique in those notebooks. Each one has a dedicated course lesson, which serves a specific purpose through the applied project.

We keep it super practical by teaching throughout the course to replicate our very own Research Agent and Writing Workflow, giving all the techniques we've ever tried (including some we didn't keep in the final version but that can be relevant).

Then, the student's goal is to build and deploy their own MCP server, implementing the most advanced agent patterns, evaluation systems, and observability techniques, which becomes an amazing portfolio project.

If, after reading this, you feel it might be a course for you, check it out here: https://lnkd.in/eBCqSjMc
And the (free) notebooks here: https://lnkd.in/ecN7kMvK
