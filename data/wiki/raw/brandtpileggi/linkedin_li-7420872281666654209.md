My second company kickoff in the books. Today we learned where Databricks is headed:

From Lakebase separating compute and storage so teams can scale freely without getting locked in, to Genie making data actually usable for everyone (not just engineers) by understanding business context and staying governed through Unity Catalog. And then there’s Agent Bricks...which tackles the “agent sprawl” problem by letting teams run and manage agents securely in one place.

Everything ties back to one core idea: we're helping teams move faster, stay secure, and actually trust the data and AI they’re building on.

Grateful to be learning in an environment that’s pushing this kind of innovation forward.
