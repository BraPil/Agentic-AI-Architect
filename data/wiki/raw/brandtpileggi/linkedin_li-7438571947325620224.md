Everyone's building AI agent demos.

Palantir shipped an end-to-end agentic architecture.

12 layers. The ENTIRE production stack. Here's what's inside:

1️⃣ Secure LLM integration
GPT, Gemini, Claude, Llama, Meta: commercial and open-source. Zero data retention by providers. Swap models without rebuilding.

2️⃣ Context Engineering
Real-time, pro-code and no-code. Contextual data, contextual logic, and systems of action feeding the Ontology.

3️⃣ The Ontology
Not a data lake. A digital twin with semantic, kinetic, and dynamic layers. Human+AI decision model built in. This is their moat.

4️⃣ Agent Lifecycle
Agent building, orchestration, and evaluation suites. All integrated. No stitching 5 tools together.

5️⃣ Security & Governance
Role-based, marking-based, purpose-based controls. System-wide branching. Approvals. Checkpoints. What regulated industries ACTUALLY need.

6️⃣ End-to-end Observability
Every tool call. Every data access. Every decision traced. Full model catalog.

Plus 6 more layers: tool services, multimodal compute, development environments, human+AI applications, package/release/deploy, and enterprise automation.

12 layers. One architecture. Production-ready.

The framework wars were the warmup.

The real game is agentic architecture.
