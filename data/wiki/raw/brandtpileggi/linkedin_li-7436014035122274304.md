It’s called World Monitor.

Think: CNN war room × Bloomberg Terminal × OSINT stack

No paid intel platforms.
 No expensive subscriptions.
 No classified access.

What it tracks in real time:
→ Active conflict zones + escalation scoring
 → Live military aircraft tracking (ADS-B)
 → Naval vessels + “dark ship” behavior
 → Nuclear facilities worldwide
 → Protests, sanctions, internet outages, satellite fire detection
 → 220+ military bases (9 countries)

• protests
• news velocity
• structural risk
