I am honored, humbled, scared, and excited that the script I wrote, "Dante Inferno" was selected and is the featured first in a new and inspiring series of table reads launching this Sunday March 29th at the brand new Wisconsin Production Collective. A group of talented actors will gather, read, and perform the script. Following that I will receive a focused session of notes to help strengthen my work.

It already is a vulnerable experience but I am curious to actually hear the characters that have rattled around in my brain come to life.
