Making a video game is hard!

As I claw my way to the finish line, I can't help but reflect a bit on the process of getting to where I am, how my expectations have shifted, and how proud I am of the game. It's not done yet, and one of the hardest parts has been realizing over and over again that I've still got more work to do-- that my self-imposed deadlines and goals continue to not be met.

I had hoped by the middle of this month to be at a certain point, but that doesn't seem likely to happen anymore. I am closer than I was to that goal when I set it, though, and I have been working hard at it.

I can't wait to share the final game when it's done, whenever exactly that may be.

In the meantime if you're curious, there is a demo available over on Steam: https://lnkd.in/gD6Qq9xG
