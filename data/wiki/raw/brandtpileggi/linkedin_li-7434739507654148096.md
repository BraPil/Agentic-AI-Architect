North Carolina, come on and raise up!!

We’re excited to share that ANS has officially opened a new office in Raleigh, North Carolina. This marks an important step in our intentional growth across key markets and strengthens how we support our clients.

Raleigh is a natural fit for us. It’s already home to several ANS team members, sits in the heart of the Research Triangle near NC State, Duke, and UNC-Chapel Hill, and brings us closer to clients we work with today and those we’re excited to build with next.

Our Raleigh office is a shared coworking space at The Loading Dock, designed to support flexible, scalable growth for our Civil and Structural Engineering teams. This model allows us to grow thoughtfully while staying nimble and responsive to the needs of our people and clients.

A huge thank-you to Aries Zamora and her team for guiding the process, and to The Loading Dock for the incredible space. We’re already feeling at home, and the aesthetic vibes are unmistakably ANS 😉

Here’s to new roots, new opportunities, and GOOOOO TEAM ANS RALEIGH!!
