I came out to my mom as trans nonbinary at 34. 
She asked about going on a family trip in 2027 where I may need my passport and I said “well I’d have to surrender my X License and Birth certificate to even get a passport now” and she said “oh, why does it say X” 
“Because I had it changed to reflect who I am”
“I guess this is how I find out who you are”
🤣☠️ ✨

EDIT: I understand some of you do not understand my choice to discuss this publicly or my tone towards my mother but do understand we have a unique and sensitive relationship that is very okay with us calling each other out on things openly.

Why does this matter on LinkedIn?
Jobs require a gender to apply. 
My license and birth certificate say X. 
With the EO stating there are no Trans or Nonbinary people, this issue will impact my ability to work should I choose to look for a new role. I can't even update my license or passport because they would require that I turn in my X ones to obtain them. That is why I discuss this here, because there are other Trans and Nonbinary people experiencing this too, and I want to speak up for a part of our community.
