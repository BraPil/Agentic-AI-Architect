China has launched the world's first commercial underwater AI data center off the coast of Hainan. This facility houses over 400 high-performance servers, delivering computing power equivalent to 30,000 high-end gaming PCs. It can process up to 7,000 DeepSeek AI queries per second, handling tasks like model training, simulations, and marine research. Utilizing seawater for cooling, it significantly reduces energy consumption and environmental impact, setting a new standard for sustainable computing.
