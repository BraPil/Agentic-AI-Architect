Today I'm 𝐁𝐞𝐭𝐚-releasing 𝐅𝐀𝐒𝐓𝑭𝑶𝑹𝑾𝑨𝑹𝑫. My new Rust-powered football tracking data loader wrapped in a Python package.

⚡𝐅𝐀𝐒𝐓𝑭𝑶𝑹𝑾𝑨𝑹𝑫
• Loads your tracking data up to 20 times faster.
• Loads your tracking data directly into 5 Polars (or PySpark) dataframes (tracking, teams, players, metadata and periods)
• Transforms coordinate systems, orientations and pitch dimensions
• Supports wide (each player as multiple columns), long (one row per object) and long_ball (one row per object, ball as columns) layouts
• Borrows Kloppy's clever io functionalities
• Supports the Common Data Format, and 9 data providers (SkillCorner, Hawk-Eye Innovations, ReSpo.Vision, Second Spectrum, Gradient Sports, Signality, Sportec Solutions AG, Stats Perform and TRACAB)

⚡ Please test it out, break it, and report your issues on GitHub.
