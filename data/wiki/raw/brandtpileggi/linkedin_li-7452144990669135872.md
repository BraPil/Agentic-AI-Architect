AI coding: more value is starting to show up in process design.

I liked this repo for that reason.

It breaks software work into agent-native stages like 
/spec
/plan
/build
/test
/review
/ship

Not especially because the commands themselves are new.

More because it reflects where a lot of AI coding still breaks:

not at raw generation,
but at specification, sequencing, verification, and review.

A lot of agent output still fails in very unglamorous ways.
Wrong assumption.
Too much surface area changed at once.
Weak testing.
No clear quality boundary before merge.

This is why the more interesting AI coding setups are starting to look less like “one smart prompt”
and more like workflow systems with structure.
