Combine RAG with reasoning models like o1. Search-o1 modifies the RAG to work with reasoning models like OpenAI o1 or QwQ, outperforming traditional RAG systems by integrating retrieved information into the Chain of Thought! 👀

Implementation
1️⃣ Choose a Reasoning LLM, e.g. QwQ-32B
2️⃣ Implement a dynamic search mechanism, e.g. special tokens (<|begin_search_query|>)
3️⃣ Reason-in-Documents, Separate LLM call to analyze and refine retrieved information, based on previous reasoning step and user query
4️⃣ Inject “cleaned” knowledge back into the “hold” reasoning chain (<|begin_search_result|>)
5️⃣ Continues the chain of thought reasoning to solve user queries.

Insights
🚀 Search-o1 outperforms base models by 4.7% on complex reasoning tasks
🧠 Outperforms human experts in physics (68.7% vs 57.9%) and biology (69.5% vs 68.9%)
📊 Outperforms RAG with 29.6% higher accuracy on multi-hop questions
💡 Reason-in-Documents filters out noise and irrelevant information
