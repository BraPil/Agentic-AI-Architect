the funniest part of teaching people Claude Code is that i still don't totally know what i'm doing either.

like i'll be mid-demo showing someone how to build a tool and Claude will just... break something. and i'll go "ok well that's not supposed to happen" and we figure it out together.

turns out that's actually the best way to learn it. not from someone who has it all figured out. from someone who's comfortable saying "i don't know, let's try this."

i think that's why creatives are so good at this. we're used to making stuff without knowing if it'll work.
